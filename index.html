Zoro

The Pirate Hunter. Zoro is the first member to join Monkey D. Luffy's crew, a ruthless swordsman who wields three blades simultaneously using the legendary Santoryu style. His dream: to become the world's greatest swordsman.

Fiercely loyal and often terrifyingly calm in battle, Zoro carries the weight of a promise made to his fallen rival, Kuina. Nothing — not even death — will stop him from reaching his goal.

Tony Tony Chopper

Doctor · Devil Fruit User

A reindeer who ate the Human-Human Fruit, Chopper can transform between multiple forms using his Rumble Ball. His dream is to become a doctor capable of curing any disease in the world.

🩺 Normal Form

In his adorable default form, Chopper is the crew's beloved doctor — small, fluffy, and surprisingly capable of advanced medical procedures taught by Dr. Hiluluk and Dr. Kureha.

👹 Monster Point

An enormous, uncontrollable transformation triggered by three Rumble Balls. Monster Point grants Chopper catastrophic strength at the cost of his own consciousness. Pure, raw power.
